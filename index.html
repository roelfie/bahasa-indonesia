Rules

  • Structured learning: pause, repeat, take notes, shadow sentences.
  • Guided listening: focus on understanding the idea, not every single word.
  • Immersion: do not pause constantly. Let Indonesian flow naturally.
  • Unknown words are normal and expected.
  • Consistency beats intensity.
  • Aim for daily exposure, even on lower-energy days.
